Overview

Stabilizing Performance
After Handover

The first months after handover are a critical period for any asset. Issues not resolved during closeout become operational problems, and performance gaps not identified during commissioning affect efficiency and cost from day one. AssetLens Operate Advisory provides structured early-life support to track and close outstanding issues, stabilize operational performance, and capture lessons that can improve future projects. The advisory also serves a forward-looking purpose, converting lessons from operational start-up into structured recommendations that inform future design, delivery, and handover cycles.

Shorter Stabilization Period
